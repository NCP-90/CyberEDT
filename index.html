About CyberEDT

CyberEDT is a pioneering cybersecurity startup based in Vadodara, Gujarat, India, committed to transforming the digital defense landscape through comprehensive education, robust defense strategies, and innovative tools.

We are building a unified cybersecurity platform that empowers learners, professionals, and organizations with hands-on training, real-time defense services, and AI-powered cyber solutions — all in one place.

Our mission is to democratize cybersecurity knowledge and make digital safety accessible for individuals, businesses, and institutions across India and beyond.

From awareness campaigns to professional defense services, CyberEDT is shaping a secure, skilled, and cyber-resilient future.

CyberShield Lite

A ready-to-use cybersecurity kit for individuals and small businesses.

  • Remote setup via AnyDesk (with consent) for easy installation.
  • Three protection tiers: Base (essential), Medium (enhanced), Advanced (comprehensive).
  • Protects against viruses, malware, phishing, and common cyber threats — simple, safe, and worry-free.

CyberRange Labs

A cloud-based cybersecurity training environment for learners and professionals.

  • Provides practical labs to enhance skills, test strategies, and simulate real-world attacks.
  • Two tiers: Base Labs (free, beginner-friendly) and Advanced Labs (challenging, expert-level).
  • Learn, practice, and improve your cyber defense abilities anytime, anywhere.

ProHack Assessment

Professional penetration testing service by certified ethical hackers.

  • Offers low, medium, and advanced enterprise packages to suit different needs.
  • Identifies vulnerabilities in websites, apps, and networks before hackers do.
  • Provides detailed reports and actionable recommendations for maximum security.
