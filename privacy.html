Privacy Policy – CyberEDT Education

Effective Date: 25 July 2025 • Last Updated: 20 September 2025

CyberEDT ("we," "our," "us") values your trust and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you access or enroll in our online courses within the Education Section of CyberEDT.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, payment details (processed securely via third-party providers).

Account Data: Login credentials, progress reports, certificates, and course history.

Usage Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, and interactions with our content.

Optional Data: Feedback, survey responses, or communication with our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide access to courses, materials, and certification.
  • Improve course quality, user experience, and platform performance.
  • Send important updates (account, security, or policy notifications).
  • Send optional promotional offers (only with your consent).
  • Ensure compliance with cybersecurity best practices.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Third-Party Services: Payment gateways, cloud hosting, and learning tools.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect CyberEDT’s rights, users, or security.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Data Protection & Security

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive information.
  • Regular security audits and monitoring.
  • Access control to protect stored information.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account information.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications.
  • Request data portability (where applicable).
  • Contact us at help@cyberedt.com for privacy-related concerns.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our courses are intended for learners aged 13 and above. If you are below this age, parental consent is required.

7. Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the “Last Updated” date.
